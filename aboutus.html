Since June 2011, Dreams of Millions, Incorporated has been fulfilling its mission by helping people in the community to work toward employment and independence. We focus on developing participants for potential employment.


We partner with various programs in the area, such as Greene County Ohio Means, CareSource, Opportunities for Ohioans with Disabilities, Re-entry and Second Chance, Goodwill Easter Seals of Greater Dayton, and TCN Behavioral Health, and the Greene County Prosecutor’s Office.


Our View On Employability Skills

Employability skills are the basic skills, attitudes and behaviors that are needed to find and keep employment. Employability skills can be put in three categories:

  • Basic skills: the ability to communicate, think and learn.
  • Interpersonal skills: positive attitudes and behaviors, responsibility and adaptability.
  • Teamwork skills: the ability to work with others on a job and achieve results.

Address the Job Readiness

Participants have expressed interest in vocational training, apprenticeships, and/or support with job placement. Based on our experiences, we have discovered that a number of the patrons demonstrate an ability and desire for pursuing career fields.


We focus on addressing the BIG issues that create barriers to employment, and help build awareness and skills. We emphasize key issues such as: job searching, excess absenteeism, and lack of skills. After working to identify issues, we then emphasize working to fix them, while at the same time setting our participants up for success.

We guide patrons to participate in group sessions to:
  • Create Goals

  • Review Goals
  • Understand Outcomes
  • Achieve Goals

We encourage participants to identify barriers to employment:
  • Assess previous experiences
  • Focus on the present and future improvements
  • Analyze positives and negatives Review Goals
  • Be open to new ideas and possibilities Understand Outcomes

We help participants
  • Identify employment goals
  • Identify gaps to employment
  • Design employment plans
  • Gather information about the industry to visualize opportunities
  • Determine the necessary training
  • Use teamwork to create solutions and review results
  • Build group sessions to design new strategies based on their findings, design employment plans

Strengths Based Approaches to Improve Confidence
  • We work with clients to design their personalized portfolio to help them stand out from the crowd.
  • We educate clients on how to recognize their strengths and emphasize them in their career portfolio.
  • We help participants learn where their weaknesses are and how to compensate for them.
  • We support team members taking their portfolio to their interview to show a potential employer or manager samples of their work: certificates, service the community, etc.
  • We show patrons why an employer needs them or why they should be promoted, and how to incorporate their experiences to promote their strengths.
